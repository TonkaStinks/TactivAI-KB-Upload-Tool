# How to Reset Your Windows Password

## When to Use This Article
Use this guide when you need to change your Windows password or when you've forgotten your password and need to reset it.

## Prerequisites
- You know your current password (for changing) OR
- You have access to your email for password reset
- Your computer is connected to the internet

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Step 1: Access Password Change Screen
**If you can log in:**
1. Press Ctrl + Alt + Delete
2. Click "Change a password"

**If you're locked out:**
1. On the Windows login screen, click "I forgot my password"
2. Or click "Reset password" link below the password field

### Step 2: Verify Your Identity
You'll be asked to verify your identity:
- Enter the code sent to your recovery email
- Or answer your security questions
- Or use your phone number for a verification code

### Step 3: Create New Password
Enter your new password following these requirements:
- Minimum 8 characters
- At least one uppercase letter (A-Z)
- At least one lowercase letter (a-z)
- At least one number (0-9)
- At least one special character (!@#$%^&*)

Confirm the new password by typing it again.

### Step 4: Complete the Reset
1. Click "Next" or "Submit"
2. Wait for confirmation message
3. Your password is now updated

### Step 5: Update Other Devices
Remember to update your password on:
- Your mobile phone (if connected to company email)
- Any tablets or other computers you use
- VPN client if applicable

## Common Issues

### Issue 1: Reset Link Not Received
**Symptoms:** No email arrives after requesting reset
**Solution:**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you're using the correct email address
- Wait 5 minutes and try again
- Contact IT if still not received after 15 minutes

### Issue 2: New Password Rejected
**Symptoms:** "Password does not meet requirements"
**Solution:**
- Make sure password is at least 8 characters
- Include uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character
- Cannot reuse any of your last 5 passwords

### Issue 3: Account Locked After Multiple Attempts
**Symptoms:** "Account is locked" message
**Solution:**
- Wait 30 minutes for automatic unlock
- Or contact IT to unlock immediately

## Escalation Triggers
- Account remains locked after 30 minutes
- No recovery email on file
- Suspect unauthorized access to your account
- Password reset option not appearing

## Success Criteria
- Can log in with new password
- All devices updated with new password
- No error messages when accessing company resources
