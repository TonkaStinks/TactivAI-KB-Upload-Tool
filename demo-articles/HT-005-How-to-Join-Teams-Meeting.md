# How to Join a Microsoft Teams Meeting

## When to Use This Article
Use this guide when you need to join a Microsoft Teams meeting for video calls, conference calls, or virtual meetings with colleagues or clients.

## Prerequisites
- Microsoft Teams installed on your computer or phone
- Meeting invitation with Teams link
- Microphone and camera (optional but recommended)
- Stable internet connection

## Step-by-Step Instructions

### Option A: Join from Calendar Invite

#### Step 1: Find the Meeting
1. Open Microsoft Outlook or Teams
2. Go to your Calendar
3. Find the meeting on your schedule

#### Step 2: Join the Meeting
1. Click on the meeting
2. Click "Join Microsoft Teams Meeting" or "Join" button
3. Teams will open automatically

### Option B: Join from Teams Link

#### Step 1: Click the Link
1. Find the Teams meeting link in your email invitation
2. Click the link that says "Click here to join the meeting"
3. Choose "Open Microsoft Teams" if prompted

### Option C: Join from Teams App

#### Step 1: Open Teams
1. Open Microsoft Teams application
2. Click "Calendar" on the left sidebar
3. Find your meeting and click "Join"

### Step 3: Configure Audio and Video

#### Before Joining
1. You'll see a preview screen
2. Toggle your camera on/off using the camera icon
3. Toggle your microphone on/off using the microphone icon
4. Check that the correct microphone and speakers are selected

#### Audio Options
- **Computer audio:** Use your computer's mic and speakers (most common)
- **Phone audio:** Dial in separately if you have audio issues
- **Room audio:** If joining from a conference room with dedicated equipment

### Step 4: Enter the Meeting
1. Click "Join now"
2. You may be placed in a lobby until the host admits you
3. Once in, you'll see other participants

## During the Meeting

### Basic Controls
- **Mute/Unmute:** Click microphone icon or press Ctrl+Shift+M
- **Camera on/off:** Click camera icon or press Ctrl+Shift+O
- **Share screen:** Click share icon or press Ctrl+Shift+E
- **Raise hand:** Click hand icon to get attention
- **Chat:** Click chat icon to send messages
- **Leave:** Click red "Leave" button when done

### Screen Sharing
1. Click the Share icon
2. Choose what to share:
   - Entire screen
   - Specific window
   - PowerPoint file
3. Click "Share"
4. Click "Stop sharing" when done

## Common Issues

### Issue 1: No Audio
**Symptoms:** Can't hear others or they can't hear you
**Solution:**
- Check that your microphone isn't muted (look for red line through mic icon)
- Click the three dots > Device settings > verify correct mic/speaker selected
- Try leaving and rejoining the meeting
- Check your computer's volume isn't muted

### Issue 2: No Video
**Symptoms:** Camera won't turn on
**Solution:**
- Check if another app is using your camera
- Verify camera permissions: Settings > Privacy > Camera > Allow Teams
- Try a different USB port if external camera
- Restart Teams

### Issue 3: Can't Join Meeting
**Symptoms:** Link doesn't work or meeting not found
**Solution:**
- Check that the meeting time is correct (time zone issues)
- Ask the organizer to resend the invite
- Try joining from the web at teams.microsoft.com
- Copy and paste the link instead of clicking it

### Issue 4: Poor Video Quality
**Symptoms:** Video is choppy or freezing
**Solution:**
- Turn off your video to save bandwidth
- Close other applications using internet
- Move closer to your WiFi router
- Connect via ethernet if possible

## Escalation Triggers
- Repeatedly cannot join meetings
- Audio/video issues persist after troubleshooting
- Need Teams installed or permissions configured

## Success Criteria
- Successfully joined the meeting
- Can hear other participants
- Others can hear you
- Video working if needed
