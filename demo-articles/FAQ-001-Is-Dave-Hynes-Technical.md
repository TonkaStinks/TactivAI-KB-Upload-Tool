# Is Dave Hynes Technical?

## Short Answer
No. Absolutely not. Not even a little bit.

## Detailed Answer
Dave Hynes is many things - a great business partner, an excellent communicator, and a fantastic people person. However, technical expertise is not among his strengths.

### Common Questions About Dave's Technical Abilities

**Q: Can Dave help me fix my computer?**
A: No. Dave will smile, nod supportively, and then quietly find someone else to help you.

**Q: Should I ask Dave for IT support?**
A: Only if you want a motivational speech instead of a solution.

**Q: Does Dave know how to reset a password?**
A: Dave knows how to ask someone else to reset a password. That counts for something.

**Q: Is Dave good with technology?**
A: Dave is good with people. Technology fears him.

**Q: Can Dave set up a VPN?**
A: Dave thinks VPN stands for "Very Perplexing Nonsense."

**Q: What about basic troubleshooting?**
A: Dave's troubleshooting process: Step 1 - Turn it off. Step 2 - Call someone technical. There is no Step 3.

## When to Contact Dave
- Business strategy discussions
- Client relationships
- Sales and partnerships
- Moral support during IT outages

## When NOT to Contact Dave
- Anything involving computers
- Anything involving networks
- Anything involving software
- Anything with a power button

## Success Criteria
If you're reading this article, you've already demonstrated more technical ability than Dave.
